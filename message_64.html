Letter from Mike Coyne: Paul

December 18, 2000

On Friday Night at the Hockey game Paul "M&M" Huyser finally made it down to his seat at 7:48 p.m. He informed me right away that he got a slip for his boat, and that it will be in the water on January 12. He failed to tell me, though, what year. Please Paul, what year will it be in the water? Just another example of you not clarifying yourself.

(Does he ever do anything at his job? I think not! He has too much free time on his hands. )

Michael Coyne
