Letter from Mark DeVol: What's with you guys?

November 8, 2000

To Mike Coyne: No more side bets? What are you thinking?! Mike, the side bets are where you win the money. What, are you going to be happy when you win a two-dollar pot in Guts with four-of-a-kind in your hand? Of course not. Instead, bet ten dollars on a side bet. That way you can win ten dollars EACH from Paul and Mike Stefanidis. It's a sweet deal!

To Paul: Well, I must say. You talk big, but you just can't deliver come crunch time. $107 loss last week, and $100 this week. That's brutal. And here you're the one who came up with this whole "Grudge Match" thing, and you couldn't even win that! Mike cleaned your clock! (However, if you're simply LETTING Mike win... well, I can understand that. Each week Mike drags his sorry ass to cards about a half hour late, and of course he's miserable. About the only way to prevent him from bringing everyone down is to let him win a little money. It's either that, or let him give us the silent treatment and call Criss-Cross Roll-'Em all night. So, if that's your true intent, Paul, well then, more power to you.)

Well, I better get going. Gotta write a computer program that figures out the odds of getting three queens off the top of the deck.

Mark "The Genius" DeVol
