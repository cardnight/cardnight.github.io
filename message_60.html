Letter from Paul Huyser: Sir Coyne, A Noble Gesture

December 15, 2000

Coyne,

I'm not sure why you are so intent on sharing new strategies with us every week. I don't think anyone really cares how you are going to lose your money. It's just the fact that you do that's the main interest. If I read this new strategy correctly, it's basically saying instead of losing big chunks to one player, you're going to spread your forty dollars evenly around the table. Not bad; I'm sure the table will be quite pleased with your new strategy. It's a little too unselfish for my taste, but I must admit it does have a noble sound to it, and after all it is Christmas. My only regret is that I won't be able to share in the Christmas spirit this Monday. So please give my share to the host Mark DeVol.

Thank You, and Merry Christmas.

Paul H.
