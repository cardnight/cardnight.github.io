Letter from Billy Bob: "Wats goin' on in Kaliforny?"

November 10, 2000

Wats goin' on in Kaliforny?

I've been watchin' this little feud y'all been havin', I'm just not understandin' y the hell y'all down ther in Kaliforny don't role up them sleeves and have at it. That's the way things done down hea' in the great stait of Arkansas. All them talk is chepe. And this Pie Gow wat the hell kina game y'all playin' down there anyhow?. That aint no poker!. I'm just 'bout as disgusted as kin be 'bout this here Al Davis he don't no notin about anythun anyway. I'm just so mad just kan't rite much more mi here head just 'bout ready to 'spload or sumting like that . Y'all need to git thum sleevs up git at it.

From the grate stait of Arkansas,

Billy Bob
