Letter from Paul Huyser: Will Coyne ever hit the target?

November 3, 2000

Mike Coyne, once again you've made me laugh. "Peace loving people"? Where have you been? When it comes to Monday Night Cards, these are ruthless people. The image of Ike after a big win, dancing around the room singing the Michigan fight song is anything but peaceful. And quite frankly (although I hate to see that particular spectacle) that's the way cards should be. Where would we be without Mark DeVol's pompous way of correcting our grammar, or Mike Stafanidis constantly giving us the insight of his perfect life. Once again Coyne you have missed the target. This is one apple you can't caramel coat.

Paul Huyser
