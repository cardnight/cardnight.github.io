What is figgy pudding?

From: Mark

December 18, 2002, 8:39 a.m.

Figgy pudding is traditional English steamed pudding made from figs (and perhaps apples, dried plums or other fruits) which is served during Christmastime. Other ingredients typically include cinnamon, cloves, ginger, nutmeg or other spices, citrus peel (orange, lemon or grapefruit) and nuts. The batter is poured into a mold, and then the mold is set in a pan of water to steam in the oven. It's served warm with a custard sauce or hard sauce.
