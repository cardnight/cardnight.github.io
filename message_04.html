Challenge from Paul Huyser: Grudge match

November 1, 2000

I am concerned about innocent people being caught in the crossfire (although some are not as innocent as they claim). Therefore I want to make this challenge to "Turtle Boy Mike." A one-on-one grudge match Monday, November 6th, at 10 p.m., five hands of Pai Gow poker.

Rules and conditions:
  • $20 each hand
  • dealer to be Mark DeVol
  • no copy advantage
  • no Joker

I also want to make it clear that in no way does this have any effect on our Vegas Trip in February 2001. I feel we are all on the same team against the evil Vegas empire. I just hope someone doesn't bring the average down so low that it makes it impossible for an overall win.

Paul Huyser
