Letter from Mike Coyne: Reliable Source? Unreliable!

November 8, 2000

Paul and Pepsi? He likes Diet Pepsi! THANK YOU! I have never seen a can of regular Pepsi in The Huyser household. It was your bag of Dorritos. Next time get Ruffles chips, okay. The salt is better to combat sea sickness.

I really enjoy throwing up (but only when I am on Paul's boat). I love the way my stomach feels. It feels like I have done 500 sit ups when I am finished. No need to exercise.

Do me a favor... wake Paul up!

Michael Coyne
