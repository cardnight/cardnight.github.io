Letter from Mike Coyne: New game... tell me what u think.

December 7, 2000

2-deck 2-handed Pai Gow for the players with 2 jokers.

I deal 2 hands to everyone except me. I only get 1 hand. Players can look at 1st hand and decide if they want that hand to play. If not they fold that hand and give me 1/2 their bet to be able to play the 2nd hand. They are hoping for a better hand to possibly break even for that deal instead of losing all their bet.

Tell me what you think. I think it sounds pretty good.

Michael Coyne
