Letter from Mike Coyne: Making up rules as you're dealing?

November 28, 2000

Paul?

Well, I have to admit that I am totally against Paul "Charlie" Huyser's ways. I cannot believe that he changes his rules as he goes. I think that he should keep to his rules that he established at the beginning of his games. It is his mistake that he didn't make his RULES well known to everyone. He should be humbled and pay the consequences and be a man about it. I know that Mike S. would never pull a stunt like that. Well, he IS a man, that is why! I also stand behind Mike when Paul said that his bank was only $15 at the beginning of the game he called. For all I knew he was just resting his winning chips from the previous hand on the ORIGINAL called bank amount of $15.

Phil?

What is the deal? Why was he CAPPING on me as soon as he walked in the door? I even said hi to him and he said nothing back to me.

Mike S.?

Buy a tent and put it up in Paul's backyard. Or maybe have someone move in with you and maybe share expenses. You seem to be wanting to live in a cave. But I do respect you for being a MAN.

Michael Coyne
