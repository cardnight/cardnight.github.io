Price of a new Bug

From: Phil LaBarbera

April 19, 2001, 7:52 a.m.

Last Monday Ike thought that the new VW Bug was ~ 31k. Checking the Kelly Blue Book the most you should have to pay for a Automatic with all the bells and whistles is $22,700 (invoice is $21,214 so not a lot of bargaining room.) For a 5-speed, you save $900.00.
