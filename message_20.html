Letter from Mike Coyne: Paul... Thinking?

November 6, 2000

Smoke must be coming out his ears. What are those odds for anyway? Am I really 30-1 to have a winning night? Is that what he means? If I have a winning night is he gonna give me 30 dollars if I win per every dollar that I may win? There is gonna be a new Milkshake in the DeVol house. The kind that is gonna be a WINNER at the end of the night. Is Paul big enough to back up those odds? PROBABLY NOT!!!

Michael Coyne
