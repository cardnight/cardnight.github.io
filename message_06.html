Letter from Mike Coyne: "What in the hell is Paul thinking?"

November 1, 2000

What in the hell is Paul thinking? Is he going MAD?!! Is he experiencing Mad Pai Gow disease? I don't know because I haven't been to Vegas, but I still believe that they use the joker in the deck. I think that they only use it for aces, straights and flushes. And at Viejas I think that it can be used as a completely wild card. I don't know why Paul has got to change the rules on ESTABLISHED games. Is this the only way that he thinks he can get the advantage over us? Well, I have to really think about betting much anymore. If he has to go against ESTABLISHED rules. Does Paul REALLY think that he is a REBEL? I think that he needs to take a good look at himself in the mirror and the only one he will see is his Charlie Brown SELF. He really needs to get himself in touch with the real world and STOP causing so much chaos on Monday Night Cards.

Michael Coyne

P.S. I have more to say... But I am just upset and can't get my thoughts together!!!!!!
