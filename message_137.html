Oldest Breweries

The world's oldest brewery is Weihenstephan, located in Freising, Germany. They have been making beer since 1040.

The oldest brewery in the United States is Yuengling in Pottsville, Pennsylvania, which opened its doors in 1829.


Brooke Burns

The only source I could find for the height of Brooke Burns, hostess of Dog Eat Dog, listed her at 5 feet, 9 1/2 inches.
