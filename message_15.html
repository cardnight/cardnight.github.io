Letter from Perry Connor: Weighing in on the Paul and Coin Man matter

November 3, 2000

I have been enjoying this website via a link from Mark Huyser and wanted to add my own five cents on this matter. I am really wondering if carmel fingers Mike Coyne really knows who he is dealing with. Come on. Messing with Paul "The Huckster" Huyser? I don't bet on too many things (can't afford it), but it could get as ugly as the Coyne Man puking on Paul's boat and me having to drive him home (not a pretty sight). I say to Mike, discretion is the better part of valor. Let the big guys swing and watch your blind side! P.S. And don't go out on Pail's boat unless you're chumming for the fish. Respectively, Perry Conner.
