One Card Night record set, the other just out of reach

From: Mark D

September 21, 2005, 2:00 p.m.

Last night was the first Card Night where two players each won $100 or more. There has never been a Card Night where two players each lost $100 or more.

I suspected that we also might have set the record for the most money won by all winning players (and, by definition, the most money lost by all losing players) in a single Card Night, but I discovered we were $23.00 short of the $305.00 record set back on January 28, 2002. On that night, Paul lost a whopping $215.00 (also a record).
