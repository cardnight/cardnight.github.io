December 11, 2000

Coyne's prediction of having a wonderful time may or may not be true, but there's one factor that he couldn't possibly foresee. HE'S BACK, and I think you all know who I mean. That devilishly cunning card player we all fear, so prepare yourself accordingly.

Paul
