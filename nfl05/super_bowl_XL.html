Super Bowl XL Pools

These pools are similar to a standard 100-square football pool except that each column and each row is assigned two unique digits. A player will win if either digit in his column corresponds to the ones digit of Pittsburgh's score and either digit in his row corresponds to the ones digit of Seattle's score.

In essence, buying one square in one of these pools is like buying four squares in a standard 100-square pool.
