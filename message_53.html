Letter from Mike Coyne: Monday Card Night

December 12, 2000

I must not go anymore for awhile. I didn't have any fun last night. I was wanting to leave but I knew that my car was blocked in.

At least there was good TV on last night. Maybe I will just come over and watch the TV. I don't really know yet.

The money I lost didn't bother me. I don't really know what was wrong last night with me, but I am not going to play for awhile.

Michael Coyne
