Letter from Mike Coyne: Rebel Paul?

November 2, 2000

Paul is a true rebel. I was just thinking about things last night and Paul is against everything. I think that honestly he isn't putting his boat in the water because he is going against all the regular people that have their boats in the water. Paul I think needs to just try to fit in with the rest of us all. The rest of us are all easy and peaceful loving people. Paul is just against us all and all peace loving people. I guess that I have no alternative but to root for Mike S. on Monday Night cards.

Michael Coyne
