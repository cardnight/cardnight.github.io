Letter from Mark DeVol: "Mike, Mike, Mike."

November 8, 2000

Mike, Mike, Mike. I just hate to see someone with so much potential quit. Mike, the side bet is your future. I envision a time when no one will side bet against you, for fear of being annihilated. So you need to take advantage of the side bet now, while you still can.

Look, I know you lost the other night, but here's why: You didn't take the side bet every time. Mike, you know that if three of you are side betting, your chances of winning are one in three. So if you're only betting two out of every three hands, you're going to lose every time! It's a mathematical certainty! But, if you take the side bet on EVERY hand, then the OTHER two people will only win one out of three times, meaning you'll win TWO out of three times! The math doesn't lie, my friend.

So, please, Mike. I really want to see you win. Take the side bets.

Mark "The Genius" DeVol

P.S. As for me not taking the side bets... Look, I'm a fantastic card player, but for some reason, I just can't get the hang of the sides. What wins, what loses... It's all very confusing. No, I'll leave the side bets to the experts. And as far as I'm concerned, Mike Coyne, you da Side Man!
