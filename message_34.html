Letter from Mike Coyne: Early retirement?

November 14, 2000

Well, seeing how I have been playing the past few months, I think that I might have to take an early retirement from Monday night card night. I cannot keep losing as much as I have been. I am living paycheck to paycheck right now, and I really don't have the amount of money like you guys do. So I am going to really think about it for awhile and try to get ahead with my money situation.

Michael Coyne
