Letter from Mike Coyne: "Who is this Paul character?"

November 3, 2000

Who is this Paul character? When will he learn not to mess around and play all of these games he keeps playing? Reliable source? Who does he think he is? I think that we can see through him. I mean, we all made it past the 1st grade didn't we? I know now and really realize that Monday night card night will never be the same as it used to be. I think that it will be an all out battle, and I feel sorry for Paul Huyser and the person that he has become. I don't know why he is chickening out of going against the money man Mr. Dr. Mike S. Maybe Paul is just too intimidated by him and he doesn't think that he has a chance to beat him? So he has to find a weak link in cards, someone that doesn't have anywhere the amount of money that he has? Well if that is the way he really feels then I will only be betting small amounts of money against him. And hopefully Mr. Dr. Mike S. will catch Paul in the crossfire. I am saying right now that I am teaming up with the GOOD Doctor and will do everything that I can to help him BEAT Paul. I don't really know how Mr. and Mrs. Huyser had a son that turned out as bad as Paul. At least they have Mark and Gail. Maybe Paul was left on their doorstep on a cold winter night, and the Huysers (the good people that they are) took him in and raised him as one of their own. That would explain a lot. But I guess that we will see what will happen at Monday Night Cards.

Michael Coyne
