Letter from Al Davis, Managing General Partner, Oakland Raiders

November 10, 2000

To Mike Coyne: Mike S. is about a size 10, and purple is his favorite color.
 
To Paul Huyser: Stop wasting time trying to figure out odds of people having a winning night at cards. Instead, I suggest practice! It's not much fun for Mike S. if he kicks your butt every week.
 
To Mark DeVol: What's the use of having a nipple ring unless Mike S. can show it off at card night? Explain that to Paul. If three people side bet will the two people that may lose have to pay the person that may win one dollar for every other dollar that they may have bet if their cards are lower than the person that was supposed to win but didn't side bet against the person who may have won?
 
To Frank Farrell: Twelve weeks! That's a long time. About three months I believe. Jump on Mike S.'s coat tails; he'll carry you home.
 
To Warren Huyser: I'm coming after you!!!
 
To Phil LaBarbera: Continue the good draws, my friend. It only hurts Paul, so I hear, when he whines.
 
To Mark Huyser: Yahoo!!
 
To Perry Conner: Go easy on Coyne Man. He loses enough blood on Monday night.
 
To Mike Stefanidis: Just win baby!!!

Al Davis
Managing General Partner, Oakland Raiders
