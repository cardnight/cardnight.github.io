Letter from Phil LaBarbera: Poker @ the LaBarbera's

December 19, 2000

So last night, I sat down with Casey & Joey to play some 21 and teach them the game. We started out with $55 in chips each. Joey cashed out for $320, Casey stayed about even. We played one deck of Acey-Deucey, and Casey got mad and went to her room. More proof that NO ONE likes Acey-Deucey.

Phil
