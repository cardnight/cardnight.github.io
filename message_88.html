New Year's Las Vegas Report #5

From: Mark DeVol

January 1, 2001, 10:16 a.m.

New Year's morning, and everyone is still up for the trip.

o Their dinner last night was pretty good, and they sat right up by the front of the stage where Huey Lewis and the News were performing.

o About ten minutes till midnight, they headed up to the room to watch the fireworks display on the strip, which Mike said was "unbelievably phenomenal."

o Paul went to bed shortly after that complaining that he wasn't feeling well. He thinks he might have the flu; Mike thinks he just drank too much. We'll see.

o Mike, Frank and Mary headed down to the casino, and the lowest crap table was $100. Frank and Mary didn't play much, but Mike finally got in on a roll that went about 40 minutes. The action on the roll was kind of slow (because of the crowd), but Mike still did very well.

o Their flight leaves at 8:05 p.m. tonight, so there's still a lot of time left. Good luck to them.
