Smack talk

From: Mark D

November 12, 2003, 2:43 p.m.

I certainly don't mind smack talk here at the Card Night message board. I actually encourage it. In fact, smack was the whole reason the thing got started in the first place. (Remember the Grudge Match between Paul and Mike S?)

However, I don't want the board to be host to cruel personal attacks and flame wars.

With that said, I think it would be nice if, at the very least, people started signing their names to the posts. Yes, sometimes it's obvious who the poster is based on the content of the message. But if we all sign our names, there won't be any question, and we can all take credit for (or be accountable for) our comments.

Thanks.
