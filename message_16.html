Letter from Mike Coyne: Paul?...Ike?...Is there really a difference?

November 4, 2000

Well, let me ponder this for a moment. I know that Paul is very CRITICAL about EVERYTHING that I say. Hmmmmmm. Let me outline everything that I feel are their QUALITIES. Who would be the best as a friend? Hmmmmm.

Paul Ike
Only cares about self Cares about everyone
Rebellious about everything Anti-rebellious
Criticizes poker players Never criticizes
Doesn't like people Loves people
Doesn't have feelings Has feelings
Is a bitter person Loving person
Has no friends Ike (everyone is a friend)
Hates to be happy Is always happy
Doesn't know a boat goes in the water Ike knows

Yes, there is a HUGE difference!! Well, I think that it is very clear that Ike is the better person. Paul should admit that he is also, and maybe change his ways to be more like Ike!

Michael Coyne
