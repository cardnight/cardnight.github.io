Letter from Mark DeVol: I Understand, Mike

December 13, 2000

I think I understand how you feel, Mike Coyne. There have been times when Card Night was the last place I wanted to be. Sometimes it was because of losing, sometimes it was because somebody pissed me off... and sometimes there seemed to be no good reason. I just wasn't having any fun.

Anyway, I hope you show up this Monday, and continue to attend regularly. Perhaps setting your sights on financially destroying one of us might help. Paul, for instance.

Mark DeVol
