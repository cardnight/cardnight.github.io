Letter from "A Reliable Source": "Can't we all just get along?"

November 1, 2000

Dear Mr. DeVol

I, like a lot of people, do not want to see Dr. Stefanidis and Mr. Huyser at war, for who would put all those Vegas showgirls through college? But I must admit there must be an end to this madness. Dr. Stefanidis needs to help the masses in his goal of great smiles, and Mr. Huyser, well, he must go on as well, for soon that boat of his will need tires since it's been dry-docked so long and its dreams of the Great Pacific are but a fading memory. So I plead with you, ask the good doctor to take on the Huyser challenge and put an end to this crazy feud. In the famous words of the beaten motorist, "Can't we all just get along?"

Thank You,

"A Reliable Source"
