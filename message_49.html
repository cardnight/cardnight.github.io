Answers from Paul's Birthday Party

December 11, 2000

Here are all the answers to the questions raised at Paul's birthday party Friday:


WHAT ARE THE DEFINITIONS (AND IQ RANKINGS) OF MORON, IMBECILE AND IDIOT?

Moron (IQ 50-75): A feeble-minded person who does not advance beyond the mental age of 12 years.

Imbecile (IQ 25-49): One who is congenitally weak-minded yet not incapable of education; a mental defective not advancing beyond the mental age of seven years.

Idiot (IQ below 25): A person without understanding or ordinary mental capacity; one who does not advance beyond the mental age of three years.

HOW DOES THE HOUSE HAVE AN EDGE AT CASINO WAR?

Here are the rules for Casino War. You place your bet, and the dealer deals one card to you and one card to himself. If your card is higher, you win even money. If his card is higher, you lose your bet.

If yours and the dealer's cards are the same rank, you have two options. You can surrender half your bet, or you can "go to war." If you "go to war," you must double your initial bet, after which the dealer deals another card to you and another one to himself. If his card is higher, you lose your entire wager. However (and this is where the house edge comes in), if your card is higher, you only get paid the amount of your INITIAL wager, not the doubled amount you bet. (Additionally, if you and the dealer tie, you DO get paid the whole doubled amount... but this "advantage" is not enough to offset the house edge.)
