New Year's Las Vegas Report #6

From: Mark DeVol

January 2, 2001, 7:27 a.m.

Well, our heroes are back, and they all won!

o Paul basically played blackjack and pai gow poker and ended up winning quite a bit.

o Frank came back with the same amount he left with... but he bought a bottle Dom Perignon for everybody while he was there, so he won at least the cost of the champagne.

o Mary basically doubled the money she came with, thanks to some good blackjack on the last day.

o Mike had the second best trip (money-wise) of his life, thanks to a huge roll at the crap table just moments before they all left for the airport to come home.

o They got to the airport early enough to take the flight before the one they were originally booked on (which was fortunate, because the original flight was a half hour late).

To Paul, Frank and Mary: Since these messages were just me repeating what Mike told me, please post any stories (or corrections) you might have from the trip. Thanks!
