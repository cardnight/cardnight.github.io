Letter from Mike Coyne: Men or Women?

November 14, 2000

Well, it is very obvious to me that there are only a few of us that aren't men. The men in here aren't afraid to have their names on the letters that they write. It is very sad that there are only 4 men here. Mark "No Action" DeVol, Paul "Charlie" Huyser, Mike "Refrigerator" Stefanidis, and Michael "No Sides" Coyne. Even Reliable "Unreliable" Source is getting involved. I guess that the rest of you are all a bunch of women. No offense "Unreliable"!

Proposition #1
I think that a misdeal should be redealt. I think that it is they way they do it in Vegas and Viejas too. I really don't think that they try and put the cards back in the right order that they should be. A misdeal is a misdeal, and the dealer should also ante again for doing it.

Proposition #2
I am all for having LIVE TITS at the game. As long as they aren't Mike S.'s! I will again agree with Paul on that!

Michael Coyne
