New Year's Las Vegas Report #4

From: Mark DeVol

December 31, 2000, 8:17 p.m.

Our heroes are on their way to a special dinner and show courtesy of Caesars Palace. And, amazingly, everyone is up! (Actually, Mike says he might be up a little or down a little; he hasn't counted yet. But Paul, Frank and Mary are definitely all up.)

Frank won a nice chunk of change betting on the Philadelphia Eagles to beat the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.

Caesars gave Mike two beautifully crafted champagne flutes as a gift, so Mike ordered a bottle of champagne from room service; it was just arriving as I got of the phone with them.
