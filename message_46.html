Letter from Paul Huyser: Michael Coyne, Great Tactician or Just Mentally Challenged

December 8, 2000

Coyne, is the day ever going to come when you write the truth? Or at least comprehend what you read and write? You accuse me of putting down the "Good Doctor," but you yourself wrote in recent articles, "Mike should wear a bro," "Mike should live in a tent," "put a blonde wig on anything and Mike will like it." Good grief! Coyne, I've got to tell you, these are not kind words. In another example, I never said that Coyne should wear a wig. I said color your hair blonde. I don't understand where these inconsistencies are coming from again and again. Please write something correct for a change. Once again, Mike Coyne, your constant incoherent jumbled up confusion tactics, (and for your sake I hope these are tactics) are killing me. I look forward to your next response.

As for your new game: As long as you deal it, I like it. Are you insane? I dare you to bank this game with any sizable bank and five deals. This is one way to show us that you're not a "sissy boy," but just mentally challenged. Two-handed Pai Gow will even put a smile on the "Good Doctor's" face.

Paul H.
