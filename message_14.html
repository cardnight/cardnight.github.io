Proposed rules and conditions for grudge match

The following is a list of rules and conditions for the Pai Gow poker grudge match between Mike "Turtle Boy" Stefanidis and Paul "No Money" Huyser (pending approval by both participants):

  1. The match will commence immediately following "showdown" at the weekly poker game (approximately 10:00 p.m.) on November 6, 2000.

  2. The match will consist of five hands of Pai Gow poker, win, lose or push. (Exception: The match cannot end with a push hand. If the fifth hand results in a push, additional hands will be dealt until a decision hand is reached.)

  3. Before the first hand, a six-sided die will be rolled. If the die comes up on an odd number, Mike will get the first card of the deal. If the die comes up on an even number, Paul will get the first card. On subsequent hands, the first card will alternate between the players. The player not receiving the first card on each hand will have the option of cutting the deck.

  4. Each player will wager exactly $20 of his own money on each hand.

  5. Neither player will have a "copy advantage." (That is, if both players have the same two-card hand or both players have the same five-card hand, the hand is a push.)

  6. A standard 52-card deck will be used. There will be no joker.

  7. Neither player will receive help of any kind from an outside source. (Advice from other people, reference to a "pair-pair" chart, etc.)

  8. In the case of a mis-deal (wrong number of cards dealt, card(s) exposed, etc.), the cards will be re-shuffled and re-dealt.

  9. No side betting will be allowed. We do not want anyone to get hurt in the crossfire.

  10. Mark DeVol will deal the game and will settle any disputes regarding the rules. DeVol's rulings will be final.

Mark DeVol
