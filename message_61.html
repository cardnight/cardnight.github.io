Letter from Mike Coyne: Monday 12-18-2000, 7 p.m.

December 18, 2000

It is gonna be a great night at card night tonight because P "M&M" Huyser won't be there. We will finally have a good night at playing cards. No arguing like when Paul is there. He is always starting something up. He can never get along without having any controversy going on. I am sure that there won't be any bad words towards any of us tonight as we are all good friends and get along very, very well. I think that Paul is the instigator on most of the problems that we are usually experiencing every Monday night. Maybe that is part of his game, to make us fight with each other and not pay attention to his evil ways and get us sidetracked from paying attention to him. Well, I just want to say that it will be a pleasant change without the M&M man there.

As far as Paul's comment about me telling my strategy, it doesn't really matter to me if you all know because the cards read for themselves, and if I don't have the cards I won't be in. End of story!

Michael Coyne
